About Swayam Suraksha

Swayam Suraksha is an initiative to help citizens stay aware, safe, and informed about crime activities happening around them. By providing real-time updates and easy reporting options, we empower communities to take charge of their safety. Our mission is to bridge the gap between citizens and law enforcement, creating safer neighborhoods for everyone.
